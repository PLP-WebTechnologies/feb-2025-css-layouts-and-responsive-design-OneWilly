About Us

The Lost & Found System is a platform designed to assist individuals in reporting and recovering lost or found items. Whether you've misplaced something valuable or found an item that belongs to someone else, our system makes it easy to connect with the rightful owner. We aim to create a community-driven solution for lost and found items.
